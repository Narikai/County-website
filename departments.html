Kajiado County Services

Kajiado County Government ensures its residents are rich with services that would rather not be provided by any company wishing to make a profit.

Depart of Health Services

The health of any resident of Kajiado county is profitable not only to the person as an individual but also the entire fraternity of Kajiado.

Depart of Transport

Good networked road is a backbone of any performing County Government, the goverment of Kajiado ensure good conditions of all roads in the County.

Political roles

The residents of Kajiado County exercises fully their democratic rights in electing their leaders of choice during the Countrywide elections, from the governor to MCA's.

Citizen Participation

The main source of Kajiado revenue is through tax collection from businesses and projects initiated within the County.

Depart of Education

Kajiado County ensures that there are enough schools for its resident and ensures that all learners have the learning materials to all.

Employment

Kajiado County throughits Gazette, advertises available job positions to the residents of the County and invites qualified for interviews.

Investors are always a backbone of any stable economy, thus the County of Kajiado should invite more investors to make use of the available resources in the County.

Development

Development is the daily activity for any County Government that wishes to do its best. Kajiado is one of the developing Counties with the higher number of initiated projects and partially completed.
